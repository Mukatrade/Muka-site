Burundi Company Setup Process

  1. Investment Approval → API (Agency for Promotion of Investments) approval
  2. Company Registration → Commercial Court registration
  3. Tax Registration → Burundi Revenue Authority (OBR) registration
  4. Social Security → National Social Security Institute (INSS)
  5. Industry Permits → Ministry-level sector-specific licenses
  6. Banking Setup → Corporate banking arrangements
  7. Operational Launch → Office setup and market entry

Timeline: 4-6 weeks for full setup. Cost: Starting from $3,200 including all government fees and professional services.
