Kenya Company Setup Process

  1. Name Search & Reservation → eCitizen portal name search and reservation
  2. Company Registration → Registrar of Companies, obtain certificate of incorporation
  3. KRA PIN Registration → Kenya Revenue Authority tax registration
  4. County Permits → Business permits from respective county government
  5. Industry Licenses → Sector-specific licenses and approvals
  6. Bank Account Opening → Corporate banking setup
  7. Operational Launch → Office setup, hiring, and market entry

Timeline: 3-4 weeks for full setup. Cost: Starting from $2,800 including all government fees and professional services.
