Rwanda Company Setup Process

  1. Name Reservation → Rwanda Development Board (RDB) online name reservation
  2. Company Registration → RDB online registration, obtain certificate in 24 hours
  3. Tax Registration → Rwanda Revenue Authority (RRA) tax identification number
  4. Social Security → Rwanda Social Security Board (RSSB) registration
  5. Industry Licenses → Sector-specific permits from relevant ministries
  6. Banking Setup → Corporate account with local or international banks
  7. Operational Launch → Office setup and market entry execution

Timeline: 1-2 weeks for full setup. Cost: Starting from $1,800 including all government fees and professional services.
