Uganda Company Setup Process

  1. Reserve Company Name → Uganda Registration Services Bureau (URSB) name search and reservation
  2. Prepare Documentation → Memorandum, articles of association, shareholder agreements
  3. Register Company → URSB registration, obtain certificate of incorporation
  4. Tax Registration → Uganda Revenue Authority (URA) TIN, VAT registration
  5. Sector Licenses → Industry-specific permits and licenses
  6. Bank Account Setup → Corporate banking with local and international banks
  7. Operational Setup → Office space, utilities, first hires

Timeline: 4-6 weeks for full setup. Cost: Starting from $3,500 including all government fees and professional services.
